A Realm Reborn Allied Societies

Amalj'aa Daily Quests

To unlock the Amalj'aa quests players must complete the level 43 quest Peace for Thanalan, which begins with talking to Swift in Ul'dah - Steps of Nald (X:8.4, Y:8.9).

Sylph Daily Quests

To unlock the Sylph quests players must complete the level 42 Seeking Solace, which begins with talking to Vorsaile Heuloix in New Gridania (X:9.7, Y:11.1).

Heavensward Allied Societies

Vanu Vanu Daily Quests

To unlock the Vanu Vanu quests players must complete the level 50 quest Three Beaks to the Wind, which begins with talking to Sonu Vanu in The Sea of Clouds (X:11.7, Y:14.8).
